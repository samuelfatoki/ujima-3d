Ujima

  • year: 2023

Ujima has been a long-standing principle in our community. Since 1619, Black people in America have had to find their own ujima because there was no place in society for us. In this display I wanted to show the ways in which Black folks have moved as a collective since the civil rights era to contemporary Cal State Fullerton. I hope you are able to enjoy these images and the value of community that is present today on our very on campus is reflected.
